Droplets of music

Fort 1881

    Each circle represents a recording of water droplets, tap one to play, tap more to harmonize!
    A circle will turn blue while playing. To stop it, tap it again.
    The location of the circles corrolates to where the sound was recorded in the fort
    If you want to explore the fort using this map, simply go to the link in your browser: https://milaluna.github.io/interactiveMap/
